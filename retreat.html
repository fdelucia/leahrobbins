Yoga for Runners Retreat


  Take your practice outdoors

Let the natural harmony of the mountains expand and deepen your yoga practice as we take the pursuit of balance beyond the mat, to savor and absorb the natural bounty that surrounds us. Yoga and Hiking offers the ideal combination of spectacular sights and soul-soothing rejuvenation.


  What to expect:

  • Sunrise flow and evening yoga classes
  • Traditional stretching and foam rolling sessions
  • Running on trails and hills
  • Individual coaching from a certified running coach
  • Healthy(-ish!) meals
  • Swimming, kayaking and all the lakeside fun you can imagine
